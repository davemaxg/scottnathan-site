About

The Man Behind the Bad Decisions

Scott Michael Nathan is a writer, raconteur, award-winning photographer, director, and video artist living in Los Angeles. He is a top-10 podcast regular and favorite on social media.

His debut book, The Big Book of Bad Decisions, became an Amazon #1 Bestseller — a collection of tragicomic vignettes from his hilarious true adventures as he set out to devour everything Hollywood had to offer: bad dates, worse roommates, A-list celebrities, models, moguls, and mushrooms.

Scott's groundbreaking "Confessional" series opened to sellout crowds and critical acclaim at the prestigious Frieze Art Fair in Los Angeles in 2019.
